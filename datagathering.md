Data Gathering Topics

- PSAP Demographics

  - Occupied Seats (FTE/Equivalents)

  - Budgeted FTEs

  - Shift information

    - Schedule

    - Staffing per shift

  - Special events and preparations

- Statistical Data

  - Call Volume

  - Event volume

  - Radio Traffic volume

  - Standards adherence (call answer, processing, etc)

- Recruitment, Selection, and Retention

  - Applicant conversion rate \[application to hire\]

  - Disqualifying factors & timing during recruitment process

  - Overall retention rate

- Training, Quality Assurance, and Personnel Development

  - New Hire conversion \[hire to release\]

  - Training Retention

    - Where do people leave or otherwise exit the training process

  - Wellness

  - Mentorship

- Technology

  - Use of protocols \[EMD/EFD/EPD ... National Academy, APCO,
    PowerPhone\]

  - Approaches to call routing \[auto attendant, 311, AI, etc\]
